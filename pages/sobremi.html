SOBRE MI

Design has always been my passion. I began my career studying Fashion Design at the University of Palermo, where I gained a strong foundation in design concepts, creativity, marketing, and a well-developed aesthetic sense. After graduating, I spent several months in New York, further honing my skills at the Fashion Institute of Technology, where I took courses in color theory, branding, and e-commerce.
Upon returning, I worked for several years in the fashion industry, immersing myself in trends, refining my visual sensitivity, and gaining a deep understanding of consumer behavior. This experience sharpened my ability to create cohesive and engaging aesthetics, as well as to develop designs that not only look good but also communicate a strong identity and purpose.
Over time, my interest shifted toward Web Design, a field that allows me to merge artistic creativity with technology. To transition into this industry, I completed an intensive five-month course in web development and WordPress, which equipped me with the technical knowledge to bring designs to life in the digital world. And in 2024 I studied UX/UI Design.
Since 2022, I have been working as a freelance web designer, developing projects that stand out for their creativity and attention to detail. I am highly receptive to my clients’ needs, dedicating time to understanding their goals and always seeking to innovate with tailored ideas and strategies.
I truly enjoy my work, which motivates me to put effort and commitment to every project, ensuring I deliver professional results that align with each client’s expectations and objectives.
